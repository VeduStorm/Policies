# SERVER RULES

## GENERAL CONDUCT
- All members must maintain a respectful and professional demeanor at all times.
- Disrespect, harassment, or targeted insults toward any individual or group will not be tolerated.
- Arguments or conflicts between members should be handled privately or reported to staff.
- Discriminatory remarks based on race, gender, religion, nationality, or any personal attribute are strictly prohibited.
- Provoking or baiting others into confrontation will be treated as harassment.
- Immature, disruptive, or intentionally offensive behavior is not acceptable.
- Members are expected to use proper language and avoid excessive profanity.
- Any attempt to evade moderation actions will result in permanent removal.
- Rejoining the server to bypass a ban, mute, or timeout is not permitted.
- Respect the integrity of the community and contribute positively to discussions.

## COMMUNICATION & CHAT
- Keep messages relevant to the current topic or channel purpose.
- Do not spam text channels with repeated messages, characters, or symbols.
- Avoid unnecessary tagging of members or roles.
- Mass pings or mentions to gain attention are prohibited.
- Messages must not include slurs, hate speech, or coded offensive language.
- Do not argue with moderators or attempt to publicly challenge staff decisions.
- Use clear and understandable language; excessive slang or gibberish is discouraged.
- Keep discussions civil; personal attacks disguised as opinions are still violations.
- Posting misleading or false information intentionally is not tolerated.
- Avoid controversial or inflammatory subjects outside designated areas.

## VOICE & STAGE RULES
- Do not scream, play loud noises, or disrupt voice channels.
- Soundboards and voice changers should be used responsibly or only where permitted.
- Do not join voice channels with the intent to harass or troll others.
- Respect others’ speaking turns; avoid interrupting repeatedly.
- Music bots should only be used in channels designated for them.
- Streaming or recording without consent is not allowed.
- Use push-to-talk in crowded channels if background noise is an issue.
- Do not use voice channels as AFK hangouts if inactive for extended periods.
- Avoid using external sounds or programs that disrupt the experience for others.
- Follow any posted rules unique to specific voice channels.

## MEDIA & LINKS
- Do not share NSFW, obscene, or violent media of any kind.
- All shared links must be safe and relevant to the discussion.
- Phishing, malicious, or shortened deceptive links are forbidden.
- Pirated or illegal content sharing results in immediate removal.
- Avoid flooding channels with excessive images, GIFs, or attachments.
- Respect copyright laws when sharing content.
- Do not share screenshots or messages from others without permission.
- Links to competing servers or unauthorized advertisements are not allowed.
- Personal social-media promotion is only permitted in approved channels.
- Media should remain appropriate for all audiences.

## BOTS & COMMANDS
- Bot commands should only be used in their designated channels.
- Abusing or spamming bot commands will result in restrictions.
- Do not attempt to break, exploit, or manipulate bot systems.
- Self-bots or unauthorized automation tools are strictly forbidden.
- Do not trigger commands excessively to disrupt chat.
- Report malfunctioning bots to staff immediately.
- Do not impersonate bot accounts or use similar usernames.
- Using API spam tools or mass-invite scripts will result in permanent ban.
- Bots added to the server must be approved by administration.
- Always follow any additional bot-related rules pinned in command channels.

## PRIVACY & SECURITY
- Never share your personal information publicly or privately with strangers.
- Do not request others’ personal details under any circumstances.
- Screenshots, recordings, or sharing of private messages without consent are not allowed.
- Impersonation of users, staff, or bots is strictly prohibited.
- Do not spread false rumors, leaks, or private data.
- Report suspicious DMs or potential scams to moderators immediately.
- Protect your Discord account using two-factor authentication.
- Do not engage in or promote phishing, scamming, or fraud.
- Members found distributing malware or harmful code will be permanently banned.
- The server reserves the right to report serious security threats to Discord Trust & Safety.

## STAFF & MODERATION
- Moderators’ instructions are to be followed without argument.
- Staff actions are final unless formally appealed.
- Public disputes regarding staff behavior are not permitted; use proper channels.
- False reports or abuse of the reporting system will lead to consequences.
- Do not impersonate or pretend to represent staff in any way.
- Members may not share moderator or admin messages without permission.
- Staff decisions are made collectively and are not open to negotiation.
- Bypassing moderation tools or systems is considered a major violation.
- Any form of retaliation against moderators or informants will not be tolerated.
- Respect the chain of command when escalating issues.

## SERVER OPERATIONS
- Do not attempt to exploit permissions, roles, or server settings.
- Creating unnecessary tickets or support requests wastes staff resources.
- Changes to nicknames or roles must comply with server standards.
- Mass leaving and rejoining to gain roles or benefits is prohibited.
- Using alternate accounts to bypass limits or punishments is forbidden.
- Report all technical issues responsibly; do not test vulnerabilities.
- Do not impersonate server systems or automated messages.
- Unauthorized events, giveaways, or polls require admin approval.
- Respect scheduled maintenance and announcements.
- Tampering with server structure or hierarchy will result in permanent removal.

## ACCOUNT INTEGRITY
- Each user is responsible for their account and actions taken under it.
- Claiming a friend or hacker used your account does not excuse violations.
- Sharing accounts or passwords is strongly discouraged.
- Selling or trading Discord accounts within the server is prohibited.
- Impersonating others through alternate accounts is a major offense.
- Alternate accounts are allowed only with staff approval.
- Evading bans or punishments via new accounts is considered rule evasion.
- Ensure your account name and profile comply with server standards.
- Any account associated with external violations may be removed.
- Keep your login information private and secure at all times.

## MISCELLANEOUS & ENFORCEMENT
- Ignorance of the rules is not an acceptable excuse.
- All rules apply equally to every member regardless of rank or seniority.
- The server administration reserves the right to modify rules at any time.
- Warnings may or may not be issued prior to punishment, based on severity.
- All punishments are logged internally and reviewed by senior staff.
- Repeated minor violations will be treated as a major infraction.
- Attempting to manipulate staff or evade detection will result in escalation.
- Members must comply with Discord’s Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
- Any illegal activity may be reported to relevant authorities or Discord’s Safety team.
- The administration reserves the right to remove any member for any reason deemed necessary for community safety.
- Continued presence in the server indicates acceptance of all current and future rules.
