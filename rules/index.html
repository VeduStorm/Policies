NovaCore Official Server Rules

Server Rules

These rules apply to all NovaCore communities and platforms, including Discord, Telegram, and SellAuth. Violations will result in moderation action up to permanent termination.

GENERAL CONDUCT

  • All members must maintain a respectful and professional demeanor at all times.
  • Disrespect, harassment, or targeted insults toward any individual or group will not be tolerated.
  • Discriminatory remarks based on race, gender, religion, nationality, or personal attributes are strictly prohibited.
  • Provoking or baiting others into confrontation will be treated as harassment.
  • Immature, disruptive, or intentionally offensive behavior is not acceptable.
  • Members are expected to use proper language and avoid excessive profanity.
  • Any attempt to evade moderation actions will result in permanent removal.
  • Rejoining the server to bypass a ban, mute, or timeout is not permitted.
  • Respect the integrity of the community and contribute positively to discussions.

COMMUNICATION & CHAT

  • Keep messages relevant to the current topic or channel purpose.
  • Do not spam text channels with repeated messages, characters, or symbols.
  • Avoid unnecessary tagging of members or roles.
  • Mass pings or mentions to gain attention are prohibited.
  • Messages must not include slurs, hate speech, or coded offensive language.
  • Do not argue with moderators or attempt to publicly challenge staff decisions.
  • Use clear and understandable language; excessive slang or gibberish is discouraged.
  • Keep discussions civil; personal attacks disguised as opinions are still violations.
  • Posting misleading or false information intentionally is not tolerated.
  • Avoid controversial or inflammatory subjects outside designated areas.

VOICE & STAGE RULES

  • Do not scream, play loud noises, or disrupt voice channels.
  • Soundboards and voice changers should be used responsibly or only where permitted.
  • Do not join voice channels with the intent to harass or troll others.
  • Respect others’ speaking turns; avoid interrupting repeatedly.
  • Music bots should only be used in channels designated for them.
  • Streaming or recording without consent is not allowed.
  • Use push-to-talk in crowded channels if background noise is an issue.
  • Do not use voice channels as AFK hangouts if inactive for extended periods.
  • Avoid using external sounds or programs that disrupt the experience for others.
  • Follow any posted rules unique to specific voice channels.
